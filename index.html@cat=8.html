Winter Edition Landing Page

Landing page designed for Zylom.com to promote the Christmas and seasonal themed games available on the website. The graphics were taken from the games and placed on a winter background.

Posted in Graphic Design, User Interface Design, Web Design and tagged graphic design, user interface, web design on June 30, 2011 by Leann Mullender.

The Eggcognito concept was designed for Kwetters, an egg company. They wished to sell individually wrapped, boiled eggs on the store shelves around Easter time. These boxes are meant to give us the feeling that we have found an egg out in the wild, much like we did as children.

Year: 2004
Designed for: Kwetters
Materials: Paperboard
