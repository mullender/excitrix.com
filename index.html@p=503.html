iRex Digital Reader Companion Software (study 1)

July 8, 2011 • Leann Mullender

 

The iRex Digital Reader Companion software followed the DR1000 series release and was intended to be a way for the user to transfer documents to and from their readers and keep things organized. The program was stored on the SD card in the reader and could be installed on any PC the reader connected to.

 

There was also a section where they could manage the device settings and set options for automatically downloading content from iRex servers to the device using WiFi.

 



This exact design was never release due to time and resource restrictions.

Year: 2008
Designed for: iRex Technologies, BV
